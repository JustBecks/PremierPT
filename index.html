COVID-19 Update

At Premier Physical Therapy, our focus has and will always be on the health and wellbeing of our patients and staff. With this in mind, we would like to share what we are doing within our clinic network to address the current public health situation. We will always follow posted guidance from local, state and federal health authorities including the Washington State Department of Health and the Center for Disease Control.

Currently, we have increased the frequency of our sanitization of high touch surfaces as well as treatment spaces. Patients will be treated in separate rooms or with tables no closer than 6-10 feet to reduce transmission risk. We are screening current patients using CDC and Health Department phrasing.

In order to further reduce exposure risk, we are also currently developing virtual/ Telehealth visits. We will have more information on this shortly. Thank you for your patience and flexibility during this time of unprecedented uncertainty. You can count on Premier Physical Therapy to continue to have your health and wellbeing at the forefront of our response to this current public health crisis.

We will adapt our clinic policy as needed depending on future guidance from government and public health agencies. Please feel free to reach out to any of our locations to discuss any concerns further.

Who We Are

We have been proudly serving the local community with quality physical therapy services since 2003. We have built a positive reputation with our referring physicians and patients which has lead Premier Physical Therapy to be a leader in providing physical therapy services to our region.

Our goal at Premier Physical Therapy is to provide you a positive environment to help you achieve your rehabilitation goals. Our highly skilled team of physical therapists will work closely with you and your physician to guide you through a safe and successful return to activity.

We look forward to creating a uniquely personal experience that exceeds your expectations and helps you to meet all your personal goals.
