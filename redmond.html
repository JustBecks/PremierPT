Meet Our Therapists


Ken Crinklaw
PT, DPT, OCS

Ken is originally from Hillsboro, Oregon. Growing up Ken was a very active individual and played on his high school sports teams for soccer, tennis and alpine skiing. After high school Ken attended the University of Oregon where he received his Bachelor of Science in physical education.

Clyde Arias
PT, MPT, OCS

Clyde is originally from Chile, but grew up in Walla Walla, WA. Clyde was always interested in both sports and science from a young age which made choosing physical therapy as a career an easy choice.

Cara Strodel
PT, DPT

Cara is originally from Duvall. She became interested in physical therapy following running injuries in high school and during her time running for Boise State University. She places a strong emphasis on optimizing movement and providing individualized care to her patients in a holistic manner.

Sarah Pongon
PT, DPT

Sarah emphasizes education and giving her patients a `toolbox` of personalized exercises to empower them to direct their own plan of care. In her free time, Sarah enjoys biking, paddleboarding, playing spikeball, ultimate frisbee and rock climbing.

Tiffany Foltz
PT, DPT

Tiffany grew up in Bellingham, WA where she developed a passion for physical and outdoor activities. After attending Western Washington University for her undergraduate degree, she worked for two years in the physical therapy field before going to Physical Therapy School at the Northern Arizona University campus in Phoenix in 2015. After receiving her Doctorate, Tiffany returned to the outpatient orthopedic setting.
